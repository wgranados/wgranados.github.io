Site search opens from the Search control in the blog toolbar (blog index, archive, tags, and each post). It is not shown in the main site navigation. You can also press Ctrl+K (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+K (Mac) when you are on a blog page and the search bundle is loaded.

Indexing uses Pagefind on the built site; run ./scripts/docker-index-search.sh locally after a full build, or rely on production deploy.
